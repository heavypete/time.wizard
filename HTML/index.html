Welcome Traveler

You have arrived at Time Wizard, a quest game built entirely from HTML5 and CSS (so far) and scored with original rocking music. After years traversing the galaxies, you return to your home planet to find that an evil sorcerer has made off with the SSH keys that power the Earth's solar shields and water filters. It is critically important that the keys are returned. Will you pursue the wizard across both space and time to save all those that are dear to you? Of course you will!
